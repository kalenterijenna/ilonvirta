New visitor

New here? You're in the right place.

That's completely normal. You don't need to know anything beforehand — or be any particular kind of person. Everyone is welcome.

Do I need a church background? +
Not at all. Many of us discovered faith as adults. Nobody is testing you at the door.
What should I wear? +
Whatever feels comfortable. Jeans, a dress, trainers — there's no dress code.
Can I bring my children? +
Absolutely — families are very welcome. Kids join the beginning of the service with their parents for worship, then around 11:20 they move to Children's Church with their own programme. You'll be reunited after the service. No registration, just bring them along.
How old do kids need to be for Children's Church? +
Children's Church is open to all ages. Babies and toddlers are welcome in the main service too — no need to stress if they make noise. We love it.
Do I have to commit to anything? +
No. You can come, sit, listen and leave. There's no pressure here.
What happens when I walk in? +
Someone will welcome you. You won't be left standing alone. It helps to arrive a few minutes early.
Come this Sunday.
No registration needed — just show up. The easiest way to get a feel for who we are.

Who we are

A church where the Holy Spirit moves freely.

Ilon Virta (Finnish for "River of Joy") is a Spirit-filled Christian church in Mikkeli, Finland. We believe the Bible completely, we worship with our whole hearts, and we welcome everyone — regardless of background, age, or nationality.

What we do

Life at Ilon Virta.

Weekly
Sunday Service
Every Sunday at 11am. Worship, teaching, prayer and community. Children's programme runs at the same time.
For families · Every Sunday
Children's Church
Kids start the service together with their parents for worship, then move to their own space for Bible teaching, games and fun. Parents get to enjoy the service in peace. No sign-up needed — just bring them along.
Fridays — prayer 5pm, streets 6pm
Street Evangelism
We gather for prayer at 5pm, then head out to share the good news of Jesus at 6pm. No experience needed.
Growth
Bible School
Go deeper in God's word. Available in person in Mikkeli or online. Free to attend.
